Welcome to Ace in The Hole MultiSport Events

Ace in the Hole Multisport Events is proud to offer running and triathlon events to athletes of all shapes and sizes, national origins, sexual orientations and cultural backgrounds. We offer Events for Every Body.

LEGENDARY

The Annual Ace in the Hole Multisport Weekend is a legendary event in the Oregon triathalon and running community.

TRADITION

We have become a traditional destination race for athletes all across the nation.

ALL ABILITIES

The weekend includes a first timer triathlon, a sprint, Olympic, a 10K, half marathon and a Splash n' Dash for kids
